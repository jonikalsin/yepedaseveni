POST Houston turns old post office into food and entertainment destination

published on September 14, 2024
HOUSTON, Texas — POST Houston is the newest destination for food, shopping and entertainment in Houston. The project is transforming the more than 550,000 square feet historic Barbara Jordan Post Office on Franklin Street in downtown Houston into a mixed-use space. The site includes the largest rooftop park in America, complete with a “skyfarm” that grows fresh fruits, herbs and vegetables that will be used in many of the restaurants inside POST Houston.
