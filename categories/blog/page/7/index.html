Betty Lynn, actress who played Thelma Lou on 'The Andy Griffith Show,' dies at 95

published on September 13, 2024
MOUNT AIRY, N.C. — Betty Lynn, the film and television actor who was best known for her role as Barney Fife’s sweetheart Thelma Lou on “The Andy Griffith Show,” has died. She was 95. Lynn died peacefully Saturday after a brief illness, The Andy Griffith Museum in Mount Airy, North Carolina, announced in a statement. Lynn appeared as Thelma Lou on the show from 1961 until 1966.
