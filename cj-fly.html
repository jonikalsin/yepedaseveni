Biography

CJ Fly was born in Brooklyn, NY on July 6, 1993. Rapper who co-founded the east coast hip-hop collective Pro Era alongside Joey Bada$$, DJ Power Pleasant and Capital Steez. His debut mixtape Thee Way Eye See It was released online in 2013. He and the rest of Pro Era toured with Wiz Khalifa on the rapper’s Under The Influence tour in 2013. He was born and raised in Brooklyn and attended Edward R. Murrow High School, where he met the other founding members of Pro Era.
