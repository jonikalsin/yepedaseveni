&quot;The Winchesters&quot; Actor Drake Rodger Age, Height and Girlfriend

Drake Rodger appears in the television series The Winchesters as John Winchester. The show shared the epic love story between two couples, John and Mary, and how they saved their love and the entire world.

The show first premiered on October 11, 2022, in the United States by CBS Studios, Chaos Machine Productions, and WBTV. The series was filmed in California, United States. Meg Donnely plays the lead role in the show as Mary Campbell, and Drake was the lead male actor. 

John Winchester is the father of Dean and Sam Winchester. He used to work in the military and fell in love with hunter Mary in the process. He married Mary and raised their young sons in a comfortable life. 

Later, the Demon Azazel led their life to darkness. Later, John became a hunter like Mary, who was supposed to be a legacy. He was unknown as his father left him at a young age, and Millie raised him as a single mother.
