'RHOC's Heather Dubrow Says 'RHOC's Luann De Lesseps Is 'Not Very Nice'

‘RHOC’s Heather Dubrow is throwing some shade ‘RHONY’s Luann De Lesseps way.

“Luann was not very nice,” Heather told Andy Cohen on WWHL. “[She] just was cold to me, which I thought was so weird because I thought we were going to really hit it off.” She didn’t say what Luann did to offend her.

In January Heather clapped back at Jeff Lewis who said she was not a nice person.

“You know, Jeff seems to have a lot of hate in his heart,” Heather responded to Jeff who said she was “not a good person.”

“You know, in order to have a feud you really need two people to participate. I have no interest in participating in anything like that. But I will say about the comments — consider the source.”

She continued, “I really just think the show was going in a different direction and so I just pulled myself away,” she told People TV. “I was clearly not interested in sharing my life anymore.”

She explained why she returned to the show: “When [Max] came out as bisexual a couple years ago, you know, were flooded with supportive messages and also really tragic messages from people whose children, you know, didn’t have a safe way to come out.”
