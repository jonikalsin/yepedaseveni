Everything We Know about Danielle Staub's Ex HusbandMarty Caffrey Net Worth and Marriage

Marty Caffrey got widespread recognition from his relationship with reality star Danielle Staub. While he is mainly known for being the ex-husband of the Real Housewives of New Jersey star, there is more than what meets the eye. 

He is an affluent businessman. Here's what we know!

Marty Caffrey Is of South Jersey Origin 

Caffrey was born Martin P. Caffrey to parents Joseph B. Caffrey and Janice Bennet in southern New Jersey. His Facebook profile disclosed that he went to Seton Hall Preparatory School and graduated from Seton Hall University in 1974. 

After his graduation, he started working for A.G. Becker Inc., a now-defunct banking firm.

Marty Caffrey's Relationship with Danielle Staub

As per reports from People, Caffrey and Staub started dating in April 2016 and were vocal about their romantic connection, posting pictures of one another on their social media handles. They even lived together in Staubs's home in New Jersey.

In May 2017, the entrepreneur proposed to the television personality with a diamond ring in a mansion. Real Housewives of New Jersey broadcasted the proposal in its eighth season. 

A year after the engagement, on May 4, 2018, the duo tied the knot in an intimate wedding ceremony at the Luna Beach Club in Bimini, the Bahamas. Only 60 guests, including Staub's two daughters, Christine and Jillian, and her co-stars from RHONW, attended the celebration. 

Despite a grand wedding, the marriage didn't last long as the pair faced marital issues within two months of marriage. 

In their third month, the wife filed a restraining order against Caffrey for verbal abuse following a dispute in their home. The husband also did the same the following day. But later, both of them dropped the orders. 

Then on August 14, 2018, Caffrey filed for divorce at a New Jersey court. Amid the divorce proceedings, the separation took a nasty turn when Caffrey put their house on sale online for $2,195,000.

Staub reportedly tried to get back the house from the court, but she couldn't and had to vacate it. 

Marty Caffrey Has Three Kids From Previous Marriage

Before Staub, Caffrey was married to Gail Marie Sincavage, a New York University graduate. They tied the knot on June 13, 1979, and welcomed three kids during their courtship.

His three kids include a son and two daughters. Interestingly, Caffrey is also a grandfather to two grandkids. He has mentioned being a proud father and granddad to his young ones in his Instagram bio.

Caffrey refers to himself as a retired investment banking managing director in his IG. Though he is not into a job at banks, he invests in businesses and is an entrepreneur. 

His net worth should cross the mark of $2 million, given that his 7,500-square-foot house in Englewood was worth $2,195,000 million in 2019. The house with  6-bedroom and 8-bath had a gourmet kitchen, a media room, and a sauna. That being said, an official word on his net worth is yet to be made public.

When not working, Caffrey enjoys golfing and boating while living in Brick Township, New Jersey.
