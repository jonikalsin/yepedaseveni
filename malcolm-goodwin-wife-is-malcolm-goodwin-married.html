Malcolm Goodwin wife: Is Malcolm Goodwin married?

Malcolm Goodwin is an American actor best known for playing Clive Babineaux in the supernatural comedy-drama series iZombie on The CW from 2015 to 2019. He was born on November 28, 1982.

Goodwin played Oscar Finlay in the Reacher series on Amazon Prime Video in 2022.

During his stay in New York City’s Julia Richman Talent Unlimited Program, he fell in love with acting. Later, he received acting instruction at the SUNY Purchase College Acting Conservatory, where he graduated with a bachelor’s degree in theatre and film.

Malcolm Goodwin’s wife: Is Malcolm Goodwin married?

Yes, Malcolm Goodwin is married to Vanessa Goodwin. They got married on September 14, 2005, and have been together ever since. They have one son together, Mahal Kita Apollo, who was born on February 10, 2020.

Independent commercials, sketches, short films, music videos, and PSAs have all been directed and produced by Goodwin. He made an appearance in LMFAO’s “Party Rock Anthem” music video in 2011.

In pieces for Interview, L.A. Confidential, King, Vibe, and Venice, he has been mentioned. In addition, Goodwin appeared in several TV episodes and movies with supporting parts, including American Gangster, Detroit 1-8-7, Leatherheads, The Longshots, and Crazy on the Outside.
