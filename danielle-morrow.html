Career

Morrow made her TV debut in a 2006 episode of the series Heroes. She played Nora Dershlit, the pathetic and mentally disturbed ‘iCarly’ fan from Olympia, Washington, who locked Carly, Sam, and Freddie in her family’s basement in the extended episode ‘Psycho’, and trapped them in her house in ‘iStill Psycho’.
