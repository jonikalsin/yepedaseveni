Cardi B Silences Body Critics With Unedited SKIMS Photo

Cardi B has had enough of the body critics and fired back with an unedited photo from recent SKIMS campaign.

The Grammy-winning rapper starred in Kim Kardashian’s Cotton Collection campaign back in October, and faced a bit of backlash after fans took issue with her cosmetically-enhanced figure.

On Wednesday (December 6), Cardi appeared to hit back at her haters by posting an allegedly untouched photo to her Instagram Story from the shoot.

“No Edit,” she captioned the image, which showed her modeling the shapewear line while striking a flexible pose on her hands and feet.

This is far from the first time that Cardi B has been forced to defend herself from body-shamers. Last week, she addressed rumors that she had undergone a nose job by proving that viral images of her appearance had been edited.

In a since-deleted tweet, Bardi retweeted someone who shared the altered image and captioned it: “Baby please tell me it’s the makeup making her nose look crimped up like that.” The post also included a comparison to Lil Kim and her nose job.

In her tweet, Cardi responded: “It’s the photoshop. You know you gagged them too hard when the haters start making edits.”

When the post was shared on Instagram by The Neighborhood Talk, Cardi added in the comments section: “Yeah it’s Getty images but Getty images they taking and editing. What’s not clicking???”
