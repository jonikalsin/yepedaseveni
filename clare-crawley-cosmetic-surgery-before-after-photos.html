Has Clare Crawley From Bachelorette Gotten Any Plastic Surgery? 

Clare Crawley has gotten plastic surgery in the past, and last year, she had to remove her breast implants. On July 31, 2021, she posted a picture of herself in a hospital gown after the surgery. She also captioned the picture, saying that she is thankful.

She was suffering from BII (Breast Implant Illness) and decided to undergo the surgery due to health-related issues. Clare took time to recover following surgery to remove her breast implants. She was very grateful to be able to heal from BII.

Crawley had not taken any pain medication after the surgery and she felt amazing. Her family and friends helped give her a boost when she was in moments of doubt about the surgery, reports Page Six.

Clare shared that people’s body shapes do not define them, and they are perfectly made just the way they are. She announced in early July that she will get her implants removed as she experienced symptoms related to them.
