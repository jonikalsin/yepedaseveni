All Fortnite Octane quests in Chapter 3 Season 4

Fortnite has partnered up with Rocket League to bring out the ultimate collaboration, and unlike player skins or other cosmetics, this time the game is offering vehicles for a change. Fortnite has brought in the Octane car along with the accompanying quests for the fans to finally get hold of the XPs before this season finally comes to an end officially on December 3rd.

Accompanying the Octane car is a series of quests within the game, along with exclusive rewards for those gamers who take part in this limited time event. Fortnite has already declared its final Flagship event Fracture in the FNCS Invitational Finals, and this is just a build up to that event.

The complete list of all the Octane quests are given below:

  • Fly 100 meters continuously in an Octane (0/1)
  • Hit opponents while riding in an Octane (0/3)
  • Perform a 720 front or back flip in the air with an Octane (0/1)
  • Earn 250 Points at RL Live
  • Earn 100 Points at RL Live
  • Damage car opponents in Snipers vs Octanes (0/600)
  • Eliminate sniper opponents in Snipers vs Octanes (0/1)
  • Complete laps in Octane Race (0/2)
  • Collect coins at Rocketeer Ruins (0/8)
  • Reach each Hardpoint at Rocketeer Ruins (0/5).

These quests will give out specific XPs that’ll allow gamers to level up easily and complete the battle pass to acquire the skins.

All Octane rewards in Chapter 3 Season 4

Completing the Octane quests is bound to give out specific rewards, and Fortnite has announced the rewards as the overall package in the game. The rewards involve:

  • Complete two High Octane quests: Unlocks Back Board Back Bling, the Aerial Assist style for the Back Board Back Bling, and Shot in Flight Emotion
  • Complete four High Octane quests: Unlocks the ‘Brella Beach style for the Back Board Back Bling and Octane Smash Spray
  • Complete six High Octane quests: Unlocks the Battle-car Blaze style for the Back Board Back Bling
  • Complete eight High Octane quests: Unlocks the Fifty-Fifty style for the Back Board Back Bling and the Clutch Victory Pickaxe.
