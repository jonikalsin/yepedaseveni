Are Selena Gomez, Demi Lovato and Miley Cyrus friends?

Friendships come and go, and celebrities are no different than the rest of us when it comes to best-friend breakups.

During a brief media interview Demi Lovato, the magazine’s cover star, talked about her music, her recovery from an overdose, and her friendships.

Are Selena Gomez, Demi Lovato and Miley Cyrus friends?

When asked if she still communicated with people like the Jonas Brothers, she shrugged and revealed that she only communicates with Miley Cyrus. (She’s fantastic, and I adore her and will always do so). She went on to say that Miley was the only person from that era with whom she kept in touch.

Of course, this conversation paved the way for a discussion about Selena Gomez, as the two stars were previously known to be close friends. Gomez most recently responded to Lovato’s Grammys performance on her Instagram.

Lovato stated that she was not friends with Gomez, but because she grew up with her, she has some love and respect for her.
