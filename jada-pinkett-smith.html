Table of Biography

Jada Pinkett Smith is an American Actress, Comedian, Songwriter, screenwriter, and businesswoman who became famous after a comedy series “A Different World”. Jada Pinkett Smith is also a lead singer of the metal band Wicked Wisdom. She has her fashion label Maja and has shared on the NBA team Philadelphia 76ers.

Early Life and Childhood

Jada Pinkett Smith an American Actress, Comedian, Songwriter, screenwriter, and businesswoman (52 years) was born on September 18, 1971, in Baltimore, Maryland, United States. She is also known by the name Jada Koren Pinkett–Smith. The name of her father is Robsol Pinkett Jr., and Her mother’s name is Adrienne Banfield-Jones. She has a younger brother named Caleeb Pinkett who is also a writer and actor. She belongs to a Mixed (African-American) ethnicity and is a Scientologist. Her birth sign is Virgo.

She studied at the Baltimore School. Where she became close friends with her classmate, rapper Tupac Shakur who majored in dance and theatre and graduated in 1989. Later she graduated from the University of North Carolina School.

Jada Pinkett Smith – Professional Life

Jada Pinkett Smith is a multi-talented star with acting, and singing skills, and a businesswoman. She has numerous amount of movies in her career. Here are some of the highlights of her career.

The diminutive actress got her first big break in 1991 with a recurring role on the Cosby Show spinoff, A Different World following cameos in various TV shows. Although her character, Lena James, ultimately earned more screen time, A Different World was running on fumes following the loss of several original characters and was dropped after the sixth and final season in 1993.

She started her career by appearing in an episode of the comedy series ‘True Color‘ in 1990. She officially made a debut on television in 1993 in the American Thriller “Menace II Society” in the role of Ronnie. Later, she appeared in different movies 1995 “Demon Knight”, “The Nutty Professor”, 1996, “Woo”, “Blossoms and Veils”, 2000, “Maniac Magee”, “The Matrix Reloaded”, “The Matrix Revolutions in 2003, Reign Over Me in 2007.

In 2008 she released “The Human Contract” which was written and directed by Pinkett Smith. She returned to television as the executive producer and star of Hawthorne, playing the titular chief nursing officer in 2009.

She returned for a supporting role in Magic Mike XXL (2015) and joined the ensemble cast of Bad Moms (2016) after largely being absent from the big screen following her directing debut. Continuing down the path of R-rated comedies, she also appeared prominently in the surprise hit Girls Trip (2017), with Queen Latifah, Tiffany Haddish, and Regina Hall.

She has acted in different TV Series such as “True Colors, Moe’s World” in 1990, 21 Jump Street in 1991–1993, Hawthorne in 2009-2011, “And Red Table Talk” in 2018-present. She acted in the movie Angel Has Fallen in 2019.
