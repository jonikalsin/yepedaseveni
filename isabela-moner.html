Isabela Moner - Bio, Net Worth, Height, Married, Nationality, Facts

Table of Biography

Isabela Moner is an American actor, singer, dancer, voice actress, songwriter, and ukulele player. Isabela Moner is also popular as Isabela Merced as she changed her stage name to this in 2019. She is popular for her roles in “100 Things to Do Before High School”, “Dora and Friends: Into the City!”, and “Transformers: The Last Knight” among other TV shows and movies.

Early Life and Childhood

Isabela Moner was born on July 10, 2001, and her age is currently 22 years old. Likewise, she is originally from Cleveland, Ohio, the U.S. and her zodiac sign is Cancer.

Her full name is Isabela Yolanda Moner. Talking about her family, her parents are mother Katherine and father Patrick Moner.

She also has two siblings and she is the middle child. Her siblings are Jared and Giovanni Moner. On October 14, 2019, she announced through Instagram that she was changing her stage name to Isabella Merced.

It was in honor of her late grandmother, whom she never got to meet. She also explained that she had not legally changed her name and said that it was “just a stage name but it’s got a deeper meaning”.

Her mother is from Lima, Peru, whereas her father is from Louisiana. Hence, her ethnicity is Peruvian-American. She also has Polish and Slovak ancestry. Moreover, she has shared that her first language was Spanish.

Therefore, she struggled with English when she first started grade school. Her mother suffered from breast cancer and is recovering.

Also, she considers herself more Peruvian than American. Likewise, at age 15, she was accepted into college. Before that, she was homeschooled at Ohio Virtual Academy.

Professional Life

Isabela Moner is an actress, dancer, and singer. She is an accomplished film, television, and stage actress also known as Isabela Merced.

She is notable for her role as CJ Martin on the Nickelodeon series 100 Things to Do Before High School in which she starred with Jaheem Toombs and Owen Joyner among other notable actors.

She also starred in Transformers: The Last Knight, Dora the Explorer, and Sicario: Day of the Soldado.

Moreover, she has been acting since the age of 7 years and she made her Broadway debut at the young age of 10 in the production of Evita in which she sang in Spanish with Ricky Martin.

Her film debut came in 2013 when she appeared in The House That Jack Built. Also, she lent her voice to Kate in the Dora the Explorer spin-off Dora and Friends: Into the City!

As a singer, she released her debut album Stopping Time in September 2015. Broadway Records released this album. She has also collaborated with Sebastián Yatra and Matt Hunter among other singers.

More details

She shared that she wanted to be an actress from a very young age. She was inspired by movies featuring Shirley Temple and Judy Garland. Then she started participating in local community theatre at the young age of 6 years.

Likewise, her first starring performance was as CJ Martin from 2014 to 2016. Martin is the lead role on the Nickelodeon television series 100 Things to Do Before High School.

The same year, she began providing the voice of Kate from 2014 to 2017. Kate is one of the main characters in the Dora the Explorer spinoff, Dora and Friends: Into the City!,

Then in 2015, she appeared as Lori Collins in the Nickelodeon Original Movie Splitting Adam. She was then cast as Sadie in the 2016 Nickelodeon Original Movie Legends of the Hidden Temple.

Sadie is one of the leading roles in the movie. Likewise, in May 2016, she appeared in the film Transformers: The Last Knight, which was released in June 2017.

Then she lent her voice to the character Heather in the animated film The Nut Job 2: Nutty by Nature. It was theatrically released on August 11, 2017.

After 2017

In the 2018 film Sicario: Day of the Soldado, she played the major role, playing the daughter of a drug cartel head.

The same year, she played the role of Lizzy, the adopted daughter of the characters played by Mark Wahlberg and Rose Byrne. It was in the comedy film Instant Family.

In the year 2019, she starred as the title character in Dora and the Lost City of Gold. It is a live-action film adaptation of the animated series Dora the Explorer.

Then January of the same year, she appeared in the Christmas romantic comedy ensemble film Let It Snow. She will be working opposite Jason Momoa in the film Sweet Girl. It is a Netflix original yet to be released.

Likewise, she released her first single titled Papi on October 25, 2019. She then followed it with her first music video released on November 6, 2019. Later, on May 22, 2020, she released her debut EP, The Better Half of Me. 

Awards and Nominations

Isabela Moner has also bagged several awards to date. Some of them are “Best Young Actor – Television” for her role in 100 Things to Do Before High School at the 2016 Imagen Awards

, “Rising Star of the Year” at the 2017 CinemaCon Award, “Best Leading Young Actress” at the 2019 Young Entertainers Award for her role in Instant Family, and “Best Actress – Feature Film” at the 2019 Imagen Awards for the same role.

Relationship Status

At present, Isabela Moner is single. But she previously dated her co-star Jace Norman in 2016. They worked together on a Nickelodeon show.

The then-lovers admitted their romantic relationship in April 2016 and went separate ways in November of the same year.

After that, there were rumors that she was in a relationship with Dylan Summerall in the year 2017. But the rumors died down when Dylan started dating Brec Bassinger.
