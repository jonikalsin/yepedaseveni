Art TerKeurst Lysa TerKeursts ex-husband

Art TerKeurst, an accomplished entrepreneur, is a prominent figure in the business world as the owner and operator of Chick-fil-A Waverly FSR, a renowned establishment in the United States. However, his personal life and association with the celebrated Christian non-fiction author, public speaker, and founder of Proverbs 31 Ministries, Lysa TerKeurst, truly brought him into the spotlight.

A Journey Through Art TerKeurst’s Life

Born in Vestavia Hills, Alabama, United States, Art TerKeurst is the son of Dudley and Sharon TerKeurst. As of 2023, he is 57 years old, having been born in February 1966. Art pursued his education at West Morris Mendham High School and furthered his studies at Winthrop University in South Carolina.

A Distinguished Career Path

His dedication and commitment to Chick-fil-A have marked Art TerKeurst’s professional journey. He has been at the helm of Chick-fil-A Waverly FSR since December 1991, a testament to his unwavering dedication. This establishment has been a beacon of his entrepreneurial prowess in Charlotte, North Carolina, United States.

The Love Story of Art and Lysa TerKeurst

The story of Art and Lysa TerKeurst’s relationship began at a Bible study where their paths crossed. This encounter blossomed into a romantic journey that led to their wedding in 1993. However, their marital journey had its challenges, and in 2017, Lysa made the difficult decision to file for divorce, citing issues related to infidelity and addiction.

Despite these trials, the couple returned to each other’s hearts and renewed their vows in a touching ceremony in 2018 held in Waxhaw, North Carolina, United States.

The Unraveling of a Union

Sadly, Art and Lysa TerKeurst’s renewed marriage faced another hurdle, ultimately ending its dissolution. In December 2021, Lysa filed for divorce again, announcing her decision on her Facebook page. She expressed her belief that Art’s actions had dishonored both God and the sacred covenant of their marriage.

The cause of their divorce became a matter of public interest, with allegations of infidelity coming to the forefront. According to Ministry Watch, Lysa accused Art of engaging in an affair with a woman he met on a dating site, disclosing that he spent a substantial sum on her, including purchasing an engagement ring and covering her relocation expenses from Atlanta, GA, to Charlotte, North Carolina, USA.

Legal Battles and Disputes

In February 2022, Art TerKeurst responded to the divorce petition, seeking post-separation alimony, support, equal sharing of property, and the rescission of a post-nuptial agreement. He contended that he had signed the post-nuptial agreement while not sober and argued that it should be invalidated due to their reconciliation in 2018.

Lysa TerKeurst, in response, refuted Art’s claims of being suicidal and maintained the existence of the post-nuptial agreement, asserting that they had maintained separate financial accounts as stipulated in the contract.

A Glimpse into Art TerKeurst’s Family Life

Art and Lysa TerKeurst have been blessed with three daughters, Hope, Ashley, and Brooke, and two adopted sons, Jackson and Mark, who came into their lives during their teenage years from an orphanage in Liberia, West Africa. Mark, who goes by the stage name T. Markus, has pursued a career as a singer.

Their eldest daughter, Hope, has made a name for herself as a fashion blogger, primarily focusing on housing and renovations through her social media platforms. On February 13, 2016, she tied the knot with Michael Houser.

On the other hand, Brooke has pursued a career as a hair and makeup artist and is happily married to Nick Isaac. Ashley, the youngest daughter, is also a blogger and a mother to her son Ryser from her previous marriage to David Hodges.

In conclusion, Art TerKeurst is a seasoned businessman who has left his mark in the United States for his entrepreneurial ventures and his intricate and publicized personal life, closely intertwined with the world of literature and faith. Currently residing in Waxhaw, North Carolina, United States, his journey remains a captivating and evolving tale of ambition, love, and resilience.
